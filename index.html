My Story:

I'm a Skateboarder, Mountain Biker, and Engineer. I first started coding when in elementary school, showing my classmates how I was able to control the color of the words on my school website after reading up on the power of HTML. Since then, I've been active in the Michigan computer science community by joining programming clubs, managing a quadcopter team, being selected to take a specialized Machine Learning class, and collaborating with a Michigan EECS professor to explore Convolutional Neural Networks. Outside of school, I enjoy programming small games for my own amusement, exploring the intracacies of mathematics with visual simulations, and creating solutions to problems I see in the world of business. You can read more about my creations below!
